Groups of Creatures

Most of the groups presented below contain creatures that are not well defined. They usually require some class levels or make use of broad categories of creatures. Some of them are also quite huge. They can nonetheless be interesting encounters.

The groups are ordered somewhat arbitrarily by the CR of the main creature and the alphabetically. If you are looking for a specific creature, it's better to use the Search function of your browser.
