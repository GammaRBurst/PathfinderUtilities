Do you want more personalized creatures? Look no further! There are numerous possibilities:
  • You can increase the racial hit dice of a low-CR creature you like.
  • You can add class levels to a creature. The rules can be found here, altough it can be difficult to find the creature's role. A few creatures with class levels can already be found here.
  • You can apply one or more templates to a creature.
  • You can choose an NPC from an extensive list.
  • You can create your own NPC using the lists of races and classes.
  • You can take a look at the list of vague and huge groups.
  • Or any combination of these suggestions.
